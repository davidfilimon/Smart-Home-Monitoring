# Caregiver Notification Policy

## Notification levels

### Level 0 - Normal

No notification is sent.

### Level 1 - Minor deviation

The anomaly appears only in the dashboard. No immediate caregiver alert is required.

### Level 2 - Soft caregiver check

A caregiver notification may be sent when a relevant routine anomaly is detected or repeated.

Example message:

> A routine anomaly was detected compared to the resident's usual pattern. Please check the dashboard or contact the resident.

### Level 3 - Urgent check recommended

Used only for severe unusual inactivity or a strong anomaly pattern.

Example message:

> Severe unusual inactivity was detected compared to the resident's normal routine. An urgent check is recommended.

## Safety rule

The system must not claim:

- the resident has fallen
- the resident is ill
- an emergency definitely occurred

It may only claim that the observed routine differs from the learned baseline.
